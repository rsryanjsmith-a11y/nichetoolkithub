What platforms does this cover?

Primarily Instagram and Facebook, which is where most small local businesses get the best ROI. Content is delivered in a format that also works for LinkedIn and TikTok with minimal adjustment. If you have a specific platform in mind, mention it when you reach out.

How custom is the content ‚Äî is it AI-generated slop?

Every package is hand-built for your specific business. AI is used for imagery (faster and cheaper than stock or photoshoots) and as a writing assist, but I personally review, edit, and approve every single post before delivery. Nothing ships that doesn't sound like you.

How long is the commitment?

Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No annual contracts, no lock-in. Most clients stay because the content keeps working.

What if I don't like a post?

Every tier includes two rounds of revisions on any post. If something isn't landing, send it back. If something really isn't working, I'll replace it at no extra cost.

I focus on small service-based local businesses ‚Äî landscapers, home services, trades, local shops, wellness practices, beauty and salon, real estate agents. If you're unsure whether your business fits, ask me on the intake call.
